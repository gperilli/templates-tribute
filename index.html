G.P. Piranesi

Piranesi from 1720 to 1778

Piranesi's life and achievements.

  1. 1720

    Born in Venice. During his early years starts his apprenticeship with his uncle, Matteo Lucchesi.

  2. 1740

    Starts work in Rome as a draughtsman for Marco Foscarini. Starts his studies under Giuseppe Vasi, who introduced him to the art of etching and engraving.

  3. 1743

    Collaborated with pupils of the French Academy in Rome and starts to produce a series of vedute (views) of the Rome. Prima parte di Architettura e Prospettive published (1743).

  4. 1745

    Varie Vedute di Roma Antica e Moderna published (1745).

    Starts his work on the Carceri d'invenzione.

  5. 1748

    Establishes his workshop in Via del Corso in Rome.

  6. 1750

    First edition of Carceri d'invenzione published

  7. 1761

    Beomes a member of the Accademia di San Luca, opens his own printing house.

    Second edition of Carceri d'invenzione published.

  8. 1767

    Made a knight of the Golden Spur.

  9. 1777

    Publishes his Avanzi degli Edifici di Pesto.

  10. 1778

    Dies

The Carceri d'invenzione

Piranesi's visonary collection of imagined prisons featuring in some cases impossible geometries.
