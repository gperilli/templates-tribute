F.S. Surname

Surname from 1920 to 2000

Surname's life and achievements.

  1. 1920

    Hatched on the moon of Glorp. In her early years, she begins her training with her uncle, Bloop the Gelatinous.

  2. 1940

    Begins work in Candyland as a doodler for Lolly Popinski. Starts her studies under Gumdrop Gorgonzola, who introduced her to the art of scribbling and doodling.

  3. 1943

    Teamed up with the wizards of the Jellybean Academy in Lollipop Land and starts to create a series of gumdrop vistas. First Collection of Candyland Castles and Landscapes published (1943).

  4. 1945

    Various Views of Ancient and Modern Candyland published (1945).

    Begins her work on the Imaginary Dungeons of Whimsy.

  5. 1948

    Sets up her workshop in Rainbow Road, Via del Candy Cane, in Candyland.

  6. 1950

    First edition of Imaginary Dungeons of Whimsy published

  7. 1961

    Becomes a member of the Accademia di San Sugar, opens her own candy-making factory.

    Second edition of Imaginary Dungeons of Whimsy published.

  8. 1967

    Bestowed the title of Queen of the Sugar Scepter.

  9. 1977

    Publishes her Leftover Structures of Marshmallowville..

  10. 2000

    Sweetly ascends to the marshmallow clouds, leaving behind a trail of candy-coated memories.
